What your application is being reviewed for

LeasePro offers scalable tenant legal liability protection designed to reduce uninsured exposure and support full lease compliance.

Tenant Legal Liability

LeasePro is a tenant legal liability program that helps property owners and managers protect against unit-level property damage during occupancy.

Flexible Coverage Options

$100,000–$300,000 in TLL coverage for tenant-caused property damage, including fire, smoke, and water-related incidents.

Optional Resident Protection

Optional $10,000 in personal property coverage for residents’ belongings and temporary relocation expenses.

Verification & Monitoring

Optional verification and monitoring services help ensure coverage remains active and compliant throughout the lease term.
